Buying Coffee Interaction Flow

  1. Customer enters coffee shop and looks at the menu.
  2. Customer sees what he wants to order and approches the cashier.
  3. He tells the cashier what he would like to order and the cashier inputs his selection into the register.
  4. The cashier tells the customer the total of the item.
  5. The customer pays the total with a credit card by swipping it through the machine.
  6. The order is confirmed and the customer is given his receipt.
  7. The order is relayed to the back where an employee makes the item.
  8. Once finished, the employee marks the item has been completed and brings it to the counter for the customer.
  9. The item is given to the customer, completing the transaction.
